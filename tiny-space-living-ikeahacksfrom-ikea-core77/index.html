Tiny Space Living: Ikeahacks...from Ikea - Core77

2017-08-17 By Teejay

I've always been a bit of a design nerd ... but not enough to actually be a designer or artist. At any rate, I do love good (and fun) design. I'm also fascinated with what people are able to do in small spaces. These Ikea "Square Meter Challenges" are amazing.

I came across this interesting series of videos in Ikea's archives. Called the "Square Meter Challenge," Ikea's designers were tasked with using Ikea products to create livable, functional, comfortable space in some seriously tiny footprints.
