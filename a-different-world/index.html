A different world

2017-09-11 By Teejay

Note: I posted this originally on the Friendface ... but felt I should post it here too.

Found this photo on imgur. People watching the WTC burn 16 years ago.

I remember that day pretty vividly. I remember feeling like the world would change. And it has.

I hope we can all pull ourselves up by the collective bootstraps and learn to be nicer to each other. We're stuck on this planet together, we might as well.

Hug your loved ones. Say "hi" to your neighbors. Reach out to somebody who needs a hand right now. Stay safe.

Love you guys.
