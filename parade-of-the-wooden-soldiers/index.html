Parade of the Wooden Soldiers

2017-09-04 By Teejay

I went to Arlington National Cemetery to visit my Oma today. She passed away almost seven months ago. I got much more emotional than I expected to, if I'm honest. But her headstone is complete now ... so it was a very nice visit.

There are things that keep popping up that remind me of her lately too ... like she's letting me know she's still around. The one that warmed my heart most recently was an article about a collection of 78rpm records being digitized on archive.org. I was randomly searching the archive and stumbled upon "Parade of the Wooden Soldiers." Oma used to play that song for me, back when she had her piano. Now I have her piano ... and the sheet music for "Parade of the Wooden Soldiers." Edit: I can't seem to get the embed to work, so just head over and listen to the song, dagnabit!

I think I need to learn that song ...
